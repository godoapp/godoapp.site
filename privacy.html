01 — Introduction

About This Policy

GoDo ("we", "us", "our") operates a mobile application designed to encourage daily creative expression through photography challenges.


This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it. By using GoDo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

02 — Data Collection

What We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Username or profile name
  • Email address
  • Photos you upload
  • Optional profile details

Content you create:

  • Images submitted to challenges
  • Captions or descriptions
  • Interactions (likes, views, participation)

Automatically collected:

  • Device information (model, OS, app version)
  • Usage data
  • IP address (for security and analytics)

Permissions: Camera and photo library access — only when you explicitly allow it.

03 — Usage

How We Use Your Information

  • Run the daily challenge system
  • Display your content to other users
  • Show different perspectives on the same challenge
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Detect abuse, spam, or misuse
  • Communicate important updates
04 — Content Rights

📸 Marketing Use of Your Photos

GoDo is a community platform — your creativity is at the heart of everything we do. During registration, users are given the option to grant GoDo a license to use their uploaded photos for promotional and marketing purposes.

What this means in practice: If you opt in during sign-up, we may feature your photos in our social media, in-app highlights, promotional materials, or advertising campaigns. Your username will always be credited.

Your consent is voluntary. You can opt in or out during registration. This choice applies to your entire account.


Withdrawing consent: You may withdraw at any time by contacting [email protected]. Withdrawal does not affect prior use made before withdrawal.


What we will never do: We will never sell your photos to third parties for their commercial use, use your content in a misleading context, or remove your credit/attribution.

05 — Sharing

How Your Content Is Shared

GoDo is a social platform by design. When you upload content, your photos may be visible to other users, your username is associated with your content, and your submissions may be featured or highlighted. You control what you share.

06 — Third Parties

Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with analytics providers, cloud storage providers, and security tools. These partners are required to handle your data securely.

07 — Retention

Data Retention

  • Account data → until you delete your account
  • Uploaded content → until you delete it or your account
  • Analytics data → anonymized or periodically deleted

You can request deletion at any time.
