Frequently Asked Questions

📅 How does the daily challenge work?▼
Every day at midnight, a new photography challenge is unlocked. You have 24 hours to take a photo, upload it, and see how others interpreted the same prompt. Challenges reset daily — so come back every day!
🖼️ Can I edit or delete my uploaded photo?▼
Yes! You can delete your uploaded photo directly from the app at any time. Once deleted, it is permanently removed from our servers. Currently, replacing a photo requires deleting the old one first.
📸 Who can see my photos?▼
Photos you submit to a challenge are visible to all GoDo users participating in that challenge. Your username is shown alongside your submission. You control what you upload — if you don't upload, nothing is shared.
🎨 Can GoDo use my photos for promotion?▼
Only if you opted in during registration. If you gave consent, we may feature your photos in our social media or promotional content — always with your username credited. You can withdraw consent at any time by emailing us. We never sell your photos to third parties.
🗑️ How do I delete my account?▼
You can delete your account from the Settings menu inside the app. Once deleted, all your personal data and uploaded content will be permanently removed. This action cannot be undone.
🔞 Is GoDo available for children?▼
GoDo is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately.
🇪🇺 I'm in the EU — what are my rights?▼
Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to withdraw any consent you've given. Simply email us at godoapp.keve@gmail.com and we'll handle your request promptly.
🐛 I found a bug. How do I report it?▼
We'd love to hear about it! Email us at godoapp.keve@gmail.com with a short description of what happened, your device model, and your iOS version. Screenshots are a huge help. Thank you for making GoDo better!
